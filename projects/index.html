MLB-Menu

MLB Menu is a small, simple little mac app that reads in some JSON from MLB to show the current games, their scores, and give you quick access to the Gameday pages by clickingon them. It's these sorts of little programs and scripts, that provide some quick payoff and tangable benefit, that remind me how much I like programming sometimes.
